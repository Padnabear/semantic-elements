Panda Facts.

Below is a list of 3 facts about giant pandas stomach and diet.

  • Giant pandas stomachs are better suited for digesting meat.
  • Giant pandas diets consist of mostly bamboo.
  • They can eat between 26 to 84 pounds of bamboo a day!

Giant pandas will only care for one offspring. If they have multiple twins the zookeepers will swap the babys so the pandas think they are raising only one.

The scientific name for giant pandas is Ailuropoda melanoleuca

Penguin Facts

The tallest penguin species currently living is the Aptenodytes forsteri also known as emperor penguins

All species of penguin live in the southern hemisphere.

The scientific name for penguins is Spheniscidae.
